About Us

Island nostalgia baked into every layer.

Serendib Bakehouse began as a family-run Colombo corner shop in 1989. Today we knead, laminate, and sugar-coat memories for a new city while honoring the rituals that nurtured us.

Heritage

Recipes passed down by achchi (grandmothers), modernized with seasonal produce.

Craft

Each bake is hand-scored, brushed, and garnished—never rushed.

Community

We mentor young pastry chefs and invest in village spice co-ops.

Milestones

Journey from Cinnamon Gardens to your neighborhood.

  1. 1989

    Doorstep bakery opens in Colombo, named after the island’s poetic moniker—Serendib.

  2. 2006

    Introduced traveling tea trolley catering, bringing short eats to office corridors.

  3. 2018

    Expanded to boutique production kitchen abroad, shipping celebration cakes across the region.

  4. Today

    Pop-up bake bars, tasting menus, and immersive Sri Lankan dessert workshops.
