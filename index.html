Island-born & oven-finished

Handcrafted Sri Lankan bakes inspired by the warmth of Serendib.

Serendib—an ancient name for the island of Sri Lanka, carried by travellers and whispered in old stories—speaks of wonder, warmth, and discovery. From Colombo to Jaffna, our bakes honour that shared past: layers of fragrant spice, gentle sweetness, and the lingering glow of slow‑building heat. These are flavours shaped by centuries, remembered in kitchens, and carried home in every bite.
